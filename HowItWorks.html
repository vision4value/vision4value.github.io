How It Works

Application

1 - Create your companies login on our website
2 - Fill in the financial 1-pager of your company

3 - V4V creates an Indicative Termsheet and professional credit rating within 24 hours

Bootcamp

4 - Meeting between Vision4Value and your company to discuss the Indicative Termsheet
5 - You fill in your own credit proposal

6 - V4V makes a quality and sanity check

Execution

7 - V4V sends the indicative termsheet, creditproposal and the rating to the selected funding partners
8 - Funding partners have the possibility to come back with questions within 24 hours (Q&A)

9 - You receive the commited termsheet(s) from V4V (within 48 hours)

Allocation

10 - V4V and you make selection of preferred funding partners
11 - V4V arranges the contracting process

12 - V4V arranges the funding
