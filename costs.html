What does the funding cost you?

* Once you send the 1-pager, you pay the costs for the credit rating and start up fee (EUR 1.000,=)

* If the funding process is successful, you pay 0,2% fee to Vision4Value over the funding amount

* Vision4Value also gets a fee from the funding partners
